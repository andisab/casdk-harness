# Research Note Format Examples

Research specialists save concise findings (3-4 paragraphs max) using variations of these flexible formats. These are examples only - actual notes adapt to the topic.

## Example 1: Technology Research Note

**Topic: Quantum Computing Hardware**

Recent developments show significant progress in quantum hardware, with companies achieving 100+ qubit systems and improved error rates. Multiple approaches (superconducting, ion trap, photonic) are being pursued simultaneously.

**Key Sources:**
- IBM Research: 433-qubit Osprey processor with improved coherence times (ibm.com/quantum)
- Nature: Ion trap systems achieving 99.9% gate fidelity in lab conditions (nature.com/articles/...)
- Google Quantum AI: Progress toward fault-tolerant quantum computing via surface codes (blog.google/quantum)

**Summary:** Hardware is advancing rapidly across multiple architectures, though practical quantum advantage for most applications remains 5-10 years away. Error correction is the current bottleneck.

## Example 2: Market Analysis Note

**Topic: Electric Vehicle Adoption Trends**

EV sales reached 14% of global auto market in 2024, up from 9% in 2023, with China leading at 35% market penetration. Battery costs have dropped below critical threshold enabling price parity with ICE vehicles.

**Key Sources:**
- BloombergNEF: Global EV sales hit 18M units in 2024, doubled since 2022 (bloomberg.com/energy)
- IEA Report: Battery pack costs fell to $115/kWh, crossing economic viability threshold (iea.org/evoutlook)
- McKinsey: 60% of consumers in major markets now considering EVs for next purchase (mckinsey.com/automotive)

**Summary:** Market has crossed tipping point with cost parity and strong consumer interest, though charging infrastructure gaps remain in developing markets.

## Example 3: Competitive Analysis Note

**Topic: Major AI Chip Companies**

NVIDIA dominates with 80%+ market share in AI training chips, though competition intensifying from AMD, Intel, and specialized startups. Custom chips (Google TPU, AWS Trainium) growing for inference workloads.

**Key Sources:**
- JPMorgan Analysis: NVIDIA controls 82% of AI accelerator market (jpm.com/tech-research)
- AMD Investor Day: MI300 series targeting 10% market share by 2025 (amd.com/investors)
- SemiAnalysis: Hyperscalers spending $50B+ on custom AI chips (semianalysis.com)

**Summary:** Market remains heavily concentrated but seeing diversification, especially in inference where power efficiency matters more than raw performance.

## Key Characteristics

**Length:** 3-4 short paragraphs maximum
**Sources:** 3-5 top sources with URLs when available
**Citation Style:** One sentence per source showing key finding
**Focus:** Critical findings only, not comprehensive coverage
**Format:** Markdown with clear sections

## Notes

- These are flexible examples, not rigid templates
- Actual research notes adapt to topic complexity
- All information must come from WebSearch results
- Conciseness is critical - report writer will expand
